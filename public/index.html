Business on Demand

A website that helps you determine the competition in an area

About

Are you thinking about opening a business but not sure about the local competition? Using this tool allows you to see what type of business are located in a given area. The following are available business categories:

+Food and Restaurants

+Clothing and Attire

+Electronical Goods

+Goods for Homes

Omnisci + Capitol One

Using data compiled from Capitol One and and visually mapped with Omnisci, aspiring business owners are able to visualize where the competion will be held and see where the demand for their services would be the greatest.
